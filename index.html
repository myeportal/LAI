FREE SESSION 1

The Ranch Rule: never let AI act without boundaries, review, and purpose.

The biggest mistake people make with AI is treating it like magic instead of a force multiplier that needs constraints. When AI is useful, it is usually because someone quietly put a leash on it.

That leash has three parts:

  1. Boundaries: Define what the system is allowed to do, use, say, and access.
  2. Review: Decide what must be checked by a human before it becomes real output.
  3. Purpose: Give AI a narrow job with a measurable outcome—not a vague instruction and blind trust.

If one of those three is missing, you do not have an AI workflow. You have a risk surface.

The 3 Levels of AI Control

01

Assist

AI drafts, summarizes, suggests, or organizes. Human decides.

02

Accelerate

AI speeds up repeatable workflow steps inside a reviewed system.

03

Autonomy

AI takes action only where permissions, escalation, and rollback already exist.
